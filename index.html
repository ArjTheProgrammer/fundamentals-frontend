ABOUT US

meet our team.

We’re a group of four students who combined our passion for basketball and technology to create Fundamentals—a skills tracker made for players who want to improve every day.

Three of us are student-athletes who know what it takes to build up your game from the ground up. We’ve spent hours practicing, looking for drills, and trying to stay consistent. We wanted a better way to stay on track—and that’s how Fundamentals was born.

With our combined experience in sports and web development, we created a tool that’s simple, practical, and built to support every player’s growth—whether you're just starting out or training daily.
